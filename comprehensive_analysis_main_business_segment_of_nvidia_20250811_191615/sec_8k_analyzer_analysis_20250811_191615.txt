#### **1. Arm Limited Acquisition and Termination**

*   **Event Classification**:

*   **Factual Details**:
    *   **When**: The Share Purchase Agreement was entered into on **September 13, 2020**, and mutually terminated on **February 8, 2022**.
    *   **What**:
        *   **Agreement**: The Company agreed to acquire all shares of Arm Limited in a transaction valued at **$40 billion**. Consideration included "$2 billion in cash" at signing, "$10 billion in cash" at closing, "44.3 million shares of NVIDIA’s common stock" valued at $21.5 billion, potential earn-out payments up to $5 billion, and up to $1.5 billion in restricted stock units for Arm employees (2020 Filing, Item 1.01).
        *   **Termination**: The parties terminated the agreement "because of significant regulatory challenges preventing the consummation of the transaction" (2022 Filing, Item 1.02).
    *   **Parties Involved**: NVIDIA Corporation, NVIDIA International Holdings Inc., Arm Limited, SoftBank Group Capital Limited, and SVF Holdco (UK) Limited (the "Sellers").
    *   **Financial Impact**:
        *   Upon termination, the Sellers retained the **$1.25 billion** prepaid by the Company.
        *   The Company stated it "will retain its 20-year Arm license."
        *   The Company announced its intent "to record a **$1.36 billion** charge in the first quarter of fiscal year 2023, inclusive of the $1.25 billion prepayment" (2022 Filing, Item 1.02).

*   **Regulatory Context**: The initial agreement was subject to "specified governmental and regulatory consents and approvals." The termination was explicitly caused by failure to overcome "significant regulatory challenges."
