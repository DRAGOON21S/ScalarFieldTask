  Item 5.02    Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain           
               Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.                                            
                                                                                                                   
On December 17, 2021, DoorDash, Inc. (the “Company”) announced the appointment of Tia Sherringham, the Company’s
Vice President, Head of Legal and Assistant Secretary, as the General Counsel and Secretary of the Company,
effective as of January 1, 2022. Keith Yandell, who has been serving as the Company’s Chief Business and Legal
Officer and Secretary, will remain at the Company as its Chief Business Officer, with such change in title
effective as of January 1, 2022.

Mr. Yandell’s biographical information and business experience are described in the Company’s proxy statement filed
with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the “SEC”) on April 29, 2021 (the “Proxy Statement”).

Mr. Yandell’s employment arrangements and compensation are described in the Proxy Statement. At this time, there
are no changes expected to his employment arrangements or compensation in connection with the change to his
position.
