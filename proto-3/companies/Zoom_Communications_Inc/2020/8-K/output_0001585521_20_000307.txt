  Item 8.01 Other Events.                                                                                          
                                                                                                                   
On December 18, 2020, Zoom Video Communications, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a blog post regarding the announcement
by the United States Department of Justice that a former employee of the Company has been charged with conspiracy
to commit interstate harassment and unlawful conspiracy to transfer a means of identification in connection with an
alleged scheme to disrupt video meetings commemorating Tiananmen Square . The Company also released its December
18, 2020 transparency report.

Copies of the blog post and transparency report are attached hereto as Exhibits 99.1 and 99.2, respectively.
