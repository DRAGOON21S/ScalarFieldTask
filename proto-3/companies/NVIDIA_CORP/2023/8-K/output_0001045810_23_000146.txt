Item 5.07. Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
On June 22, 2023, at the 2023 Annual Meeting of Stockholders of NVIDIA Corporation, or the 2023 Annual Meeting, the
following proposals were adopted by the margin indicated. Proxies for the 2023 Annual Meeting were solicited
pursuant to Section 14(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, and there was no solicitation in
opposition of management’s solicitation.
