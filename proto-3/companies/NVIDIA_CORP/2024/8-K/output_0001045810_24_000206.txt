Item 5.07. Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
On June 26, 2024, at the 2024 Annual Meeting of Stockholders of NVIDIA Corporation, or the 2024 Annual Meeting, the
following proposals were adopted by the margin indicated. The record date for the 2024 Annual Meeting was April 29,
2024, which preceded the date of our ten-for-one forward stock split effected on June 7, 2024, and therefore the
final voting results reported below are on a pre-stock split basis.
