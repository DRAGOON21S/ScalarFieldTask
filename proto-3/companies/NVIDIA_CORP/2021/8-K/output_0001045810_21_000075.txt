Item 5.03. Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year.

(a) Amendment to Certificate of Incorporation
On June 3, 2021, at the 2021 Annual Meeting of Stockholders of NVIDIA Corporation, or the 2021 Annual Meeting, our
stockholders approved an amendment to our Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation, or the Amendment, to
increase the number of authorized shares of common stock from 2 billion to 4 billion. The Amendment, which was
filed with the Secretary of State of the State of Delaware on June 4, 2021, is filed as Exhibit 3.1 to this Current
Report on Form 8-K.
