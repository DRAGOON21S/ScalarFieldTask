#### **2. European Commission State Aid Decision Ruling**

*   **Event Classification**: Item 7.01 - Regulation FD Disclosure (Source: 2024 Filing).
*   **Factual Details**:
    *   **When**: The ECJ announcement occurred on September 10, 2024.
    *   **What**: The European Court of Justice (ECJ) "set aside the 2020 judgment of the General Court and confirmed the Commission’s 2016 State Aid Decision." This decision concerned "tax opinions in 1991 and 2007 concerning the tax allocation of profits of the Irish branches of two subsidiaries of the Company."
    *   **Parties Involved**: Apple Inc., the European Commission (the “Commission”), Ireland, the General Court of the Court of Justice of the European Union (the “General Court”), and the European Court of Justice (the “ECJ”).
    *   **Financial Impact**: "As a result, the Company expects to record a one-time income tax charge in its fourth fiscal quarter ending September 28, 2024, of up to approximately $10 billion, which will increase the Company’s effective tax rate for the quarter."
*   **Regulatory Context**: The information was disclosed under Regulation FD and is specified as not "filed" for purposes of Section 18 of the Exchange Act.
